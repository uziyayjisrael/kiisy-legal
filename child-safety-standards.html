The following are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities:

5. Enforcement Actions

Immediate Actions

  • Account Suspension: Immediate suspension of accounts violating child safety policies
  • Content Removal: Instant removal of any prohibited content
  • Device Ban: Device-level bans to prevent re-registration
  • IP Blocking: IP address blocking for repeat offenders

Legal Actions

  • Law Enforcement Reporting: All CSAE incidents reported to South African Police Service (SAPS)
  • NCMEC Reporting: Reports filed with National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (USA)
  • Evidence Preservation: All evidence preserved for law enforcement investigations
  • Full Cooperation: Complete cooperation with all law enforcement agencies

6. Data Protection for Minors

In the unlikely event a minor's data is collected:

  • The account is immediately deleted
  • All associated data is permanently removed within 24 hours
  • No data is retained or shared
  • Parents/guardians are notified if contact information is available

7. Staff Training and Accountability

  • Mandatory Training: All staff receive comprehensive CSAE prevention training
  • Background Checks: Thorough background checks for all employees with access to user data
  • Regular Updates: Quarterly training updates on latest CSAE trends and detection methods
  • Clear Protocols: Documented procedures for handling CSAE reports and incidents

8. Third-Party Compliance

All third-party service providers must:

  • Comply with our child safety standards
  • Have their own CSAE prevention policies
  • Report any suspected violations to us immediately
  • Undergo regular security and compliance audits
