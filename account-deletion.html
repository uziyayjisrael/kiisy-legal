What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, bio, photos)
  • All your matches and connections
  • All your messages and chat history
  • Your swipe history and preferences
  • Your location data
  • Your subscription information (if applicable)

What Happens to My Subscription?

If you have an active Premium subscription:

  • You must cancel your subscription separately through Google Play Store
  • Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your subscription
  • No refunds will be issued for unused subscription time
